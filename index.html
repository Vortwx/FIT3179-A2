Where Malaysians Go When They're Tired of Home?

This heatmap illustrates the movement of domestic tourists from their home states to any other states within Malaysia. We can see that in general people likes to travel intrastate. With the exception of Labuan and Kuala Lumpur which they favor Sabah and Selangor more than their home state (or federal territories). As a highly populated state, people from Selangor enjoys travelling interstate with Pahang being the most visited state other than Selangor itself.
